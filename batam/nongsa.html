Nongsa

Discover the Elegance of Nongsa

Located in the northeastern part of Bintan Island, Nongsa offers breathtaking ocean views, world-class resorts, and a peaceful coastal atmosphere. It is a sanctuary where modern luxury meets untouched tropical beauty.

From sunrise reflections over the South China Sea to golden sunsets along quiet beaches, Nongsa creates moments that feel timeless. The calm waves, private resorts, and exclusive golf courses make it a perfect escape for travelers seeking tranquility.

Whether you’re enjoying a seaside villa, exploring hidden beaches, or simply listening to the rhythm of the ocean, Nongsa invites you to slow down and embrace serenity.
