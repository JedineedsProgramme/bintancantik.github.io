Discover the Charm of Bengkong

Located in the eastern part of Batam, Bengkong offers a unique blend of seaside views, vibrant local life, and authentic coastal culture. Facing directly toward Singapore’s skyline, this district combines urban energy with the calming presence of the sea.

From sunrise walks along Bengkong Laut to evenings filled with fresh seafood and ocean breezes, the area captures the everyday beauty of island living. Traditional fishing boats, waterfront cafés, and friendly locals create an atmosphere that feels warm and genuine.

Whether you’re enjoying sunset views by the shore, exploring hidden food spots, or simply relaxing by the waterfront, Bengkong invites you to experience Batam from a more intimate and authentic perspective.
